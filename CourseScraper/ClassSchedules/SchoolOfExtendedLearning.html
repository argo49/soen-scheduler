Department Note (Summer) for SEL :

Complementary University Credit Courses: These course credits are additional to Faculty degree and certificate requirements stipulated in the University undergraduate calendar. Complementary University Credit courses may be taken by any student wishing to do so. However, these courses are not included in any GPA calculation other than for those admitted into the Complementary University Credit Certificate and are not transferable to any degree or Faculty certificate. SEL 149 credits cannot be applied to any program or certificate including SEL certificates.

Independent students who are taking SEL courses may wish to transfer to a SEL Business Certificate program. It is advised that Independent students wishing to do so complete their application to the Certificate program prior to registering for their fourth SEL course in order to maximize the number of Independent study credits that can be transferred to the Certificate program. At present a maximum of 12 credits may be transferred to an undergraduate certificate program.
Department Note (Fall&Winter) for SEL :

Complementary University Credit Courses: These course credits are not transferable to any degree or Faculty certificate except for certificates within the School of Extended Learning (SEL).  Complementary University Credit courses may be taken by any student wishing to do so with the understanding that they cannot be counted toward a student's program or included in any GPA calculation other than SEL certificates. SEL 149 credits cannot be applied to any program or certificate including SEL certificates.

Independent students who are taking SEL courses may wish to transfer to a SEL Business Certificate program. It is advised that Independent students wishing to do so complete their application to the Certificate program prior to registering for their fourth SEL course in order to maximize the number of Independent study credits that can be transferred to the Certificate program. At present a maximum of 12 credits may be transferred to an undergraduate certificate program.
